## How to Connect

Your agents will ask you to connect integrations as they need them. You can also set them up ahead of time:

1. Go to **Integrations** in the sidebar
2. Pick the app you want to connect
3. Follow the authorization flow
4. Your agents can use it immediately

<Tip>
  Integrations can be shared across your workspace or kept private to specific agents.
</Tip>
