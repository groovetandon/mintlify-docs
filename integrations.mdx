## How to Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to **Settings â†’ Integrations** in your Decisional workspace
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your Integration">
    Browse the list and select the app you want to connect
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize">
    Click **Connect** and follow the OAuth flow to grant access
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use in Agents">
    Reference the integration in your agent instructions
  </Step>
</Steps>
